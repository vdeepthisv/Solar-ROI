Solar Energy and the Environment

Solar's economic dividends are easy to measure in dollars in cents. By installing panels today, it's possible to:

  • Save on electric bills
  • Generate passive income
  • Boost your home's property value


    • But solar's environmental dividends are no less tangible. As more homes and businesses across the country install solar panels, our nation moves farther away from fossil fuel consumption and closer to a clean economy powered by the sun. The benefits of this transition are enormous: Better health due to cleaner air, water, and soil Greater energy security due to reductions in imported fuel Stronger economic growth through the creation of green jobs Reduction of greenhouse gas emissions, the main cause of catastrophic climate change, global weirding, and severe weather


    Solar energy's dividends don't simply benefit you - the system owner. They benefit anyone who enjoys having a cleaner and more sustainable environment (which means everyone).
